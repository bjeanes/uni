About Us

Welcome to the Echo Bay Games Expo website. The EBGE is an annual event run by the Echo Bay Games Expo Organising Committee. Its aim is to showcase the latest software and technology and give software developers and hardware manufacturers the chance to display their products to the public and other members of the Australian gaming industry.  It is also place for innovative start-up companies to show the community what they have to offer. Echo Bay is situated 4 hrs north of Brisbane, Queensland. The Expo contains both exhibits and events such as demonstrations and seminars. The general Expo is open to the public free of charge, however some events require booking. The objective of the Expo is to bring as many members of the gaming community together to discuss and celebrate computer gaming, and to see what the future holds.

The Echo Bay Games Echo is the only place to be when it comes to Australian gaming industry events. The combination of location and industry talent creates a fantastic place to see what the Australian gaming industry has to offer. The line up of top quality companies and products means attendees will be spoilt for choice. Through collaboration with the writer’s festival the EBGE has a creative atmosphere experience nowhere else. The Expo also provides a great place for the gaming community to socialize and establishing significant relationships.
