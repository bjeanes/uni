Bethesda Softworks, a division of Zenimax Media, Inc, was founded in 1986. The company has a long history of success as a developer and publisher of award-winning video games for PCs, Sony's PlayStation, PlayStation 2, and Microsoft's XboxÂ®, and Xbox 360, PlayStation Portable (PSP), Nintendo DS, and other next generation consoles.

Io Interactive (exhibitor's site)

IO Interactive was founded in 1998 and focuses on 3D games made with proprietary technologies. Their first title was "Hitman, Codename 47", a third person 3D action game published by Eidos.

Nival Interactive (exhibitor's site)

Founded in 1996, Nival Interactive was one of the first Russian developers to enter the game market in a big way. Nival Interactive Russia is the leading, and also the largest, game development studio in Russia with five development teams in Moscow and Ekaterinburg. The company also now has expanded past Russia, and has its corporate offices in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The company has several award-winning titles in its catalog, including original titles and new editions in established franchises.

NCsoft (exhibitor's site)

Established in 1997 as a systems integration company in Seoul, South Korea, NCsoft is the world's largest independent online game company. In Korea, its hit game Lineage has attracted over 4 million active players, the greatest number of concurrent users in the world. Formerly known as Destination Games. The North American branch of NCsoft is lead by famed Ultima designer and programmer Richard Garriot.
